What Lattice Inc Actually Is

A think tank for
performance research
and architecture.

Lattice Inc is a research-driven organization whose core mission is to be a catalyst for a higher quality of life for Americans. We use the phrase "higher quality of life" deliberately — and with full awareness that quality is subjective.

"What we're really after is something more universal: freedom from anxiety. The particular anxiety that comes from not knowing who you are, what you're capable of, or where you belong in the world."

That anxiety is epidemic among young Americans. It is the silent force behind a workforce that is misaligned, underperforming, and disengaged — not because people lack ambition, but because they were never given the architecture to understand themselves.

In practice, this mission is expressed through two distinct but connected experiences.

01

Performance Research

We ask: what innately makes this person thrive? Using CliftonStrengths as the near-term diagnostic and our proprietary algorithm in development, we identify each student's wiring before introducing them to any industry or role.

02

Performance Architecture

We build the environment — the sessions, curriculum, community, and content — that allows a person to perform at their highest level once they understand who they are.

03

Two Expressions

The enrichment experience delivers the architecture in person. The digital experience distributes it at scale. Together, they reach every young American — whether or not they're in the room.

The Origin

Built by someone
who survived the system.

Ashanti Sears grew up in Cleveland, Ohio — a city that has consistently ranked among the top 10 most dangerous in the United States for over a decade. She watched the system shape the trajectories of her family and peers in ways that limited their potential. While her peers followed the path constructed for them, Ashanti participated in programs that changed the direction of her life.

Now, with the wisdom and knowledge necessary, she is building the program she wishes had existed — one that teaches students how to be balanced and whole, so they graduate with the confidence and self-assurance to engage the world as creators and contributors, with minimal fear and anxiety.

How It Works

Five steps.
One transformed student.

From the moment a student walks in on Day 1 to years after they leave — Lattice Inc tracks, supports, and advocates for their success.

01
Self-Discovery
CliftonStrengths Assessment — Day 1

Every student takes the CliftonStrengths Top 5 assessment on their first day. Their results map them to industry pathways and roles that align with their innate wiring and natural-born abilities — so the exposure they receive resonates, not just whatever's available.

02
Industry Exposure
Industry-Specific Speaker Sessions

Each monthly session features a professional covering their role's deep expertise, essential SOPs and workflows, industry-specific software and technology, and the most pressing policies affecting their field today. Students don't just hear about careers — they learn the operational reality.

03
Holistic Development
Culture Curriculum

Taught by vetted experts through Socratic seminars, field trips, panels, and interactive storytelling. Topics include intrapersonal habits, interpersonal skills, nutrition, shadow work, networking, and personal development — the practical modus operandi for optimal holistic health.

04
Lived Experience
Experiential & Project-Based Learning

Students learn through shared experiences, conferences, collaborative projects, and hands-on engagement designed to ease the transition from student to adult — addressing the silent, subconscious anxiety that comes with growing up and entering the world.

05
Alumni Tracking · Long-Term Impact
Quality-of-Life Tracking

Lattice Inc tracks each student's success long after they leave — measuring year-over-year confidence growth, satisfaction in their continuing education pathway, alignment between their chosen role and their CliftonStrengths profile, and their self-worth confidence levels. This ongoing data proves program impact and informs every future iteration of our curriculum.

Experience 02 of 02 · The Digital Experience

The framework,

The cultural distribution channel for the Lattice Inc framework — bringing career clarity, nervous system restoration, and honest conversations about adulthood to anyone, anywhere, through a YouTube series built to move people from anxiety to direction.

Lattice Inc · Think Tank for Performance Research & Architecture·Digital Experience — Experience 02 of 02
Episode Format

One episode.
Every layer of a career.

Each episode is structured to be deeply educational and genuinely entertaining — with built-in sponsorship moments, student CTAs, and content that keeps viewers engaged from first frame to last.

01
Career Deep Dive

Industry Role Exploration

Ashanti and the influencer explore the most interesting but average-paying careers that actually exist. They cover the most relevant SOPs, tools, and software — with a dedicated industry professional as guide. Students see the operational reality of a career, not just the surface.

02
Policy & Context

Expert Context Segment

A separate professional provides industry context — the history, landscape, and the most pressing policy affecting the role today. Students get the full picture, not just the glamour. This segment ensures Lattice Inc content holds up as a serious educational resource.

03
Dining Intermission

Sponsored Dining Intermission

Ashanti and the influencer step out for a sponsored meal and have a light-hearted, genuinely fun conversation — relatable, human, and designed to keep the viewer engaged between the more substantive segments.

04
Hobby Spotlight

Activity Break — Matched to the Influencer's Wiring

A sponsored activity matched to the influencer's innate character. Calm? Pottery, spa, painting. High-energy? Go-kart racing, skiing, rock climbing. A neuroscience expert explains the neurological benefits of the activity — giving students real, science-backed reasons to invest in hobbies that make them feel good.

05
Resource Spotlight

Student & Family Resource Callouts

Throughout each episode: government grants like the TEFA program, local facilities like the YMCA, health insurance benefits that cover hobbies, and continuing education programs that help students jumpstart their careers. CTAs throughout drive student engagement and feed Lattice Inc's internal analytics.

Why the Show

Content that works
while the program works.

The YouTube channel isn't just marketing — it's an extension of the Lattice Inc mission. Students who can't attend in-person can still access career exploration, hobby spotlights, resource callouts, and real-talk conversations the show provides.

CTAs throughout every video feed Lattice Inc's internal analytics — giving us the student feedback we need to continuously improve the program and demonstrate impact to corporate partners.

Episode Sponsors
Every episode has built-in sponsorship moments — activity segments, dining intermissions, and product callouts that feel native to the content, not tacked on. Brands get contextual placement in front of a highly engaged, education-minded audience that is actively making decisions about their future.
AdSense Revenue
As the Lattice Inc audience grows, ad revenue compounds. Unlike the enrichment program, the digital experience scales without a proportional increase in operating cost — making it the organization's most powerful long-term revenue asset.
Afterschool Placement
Lattice Inc is actively outreaching to after-school programs, community centers, and youth organizations to play episodes as educational content. This expands reach far beyond the YouTube algorithm and puts the show directly in front of the students it was made for.
Brand Deals
Long-form partnerships with brands whose products and values align with the Lattice Inc mission — wellness, career tools, education technology, and more. As the show builds a track record, brand deal value scales with it.
For Influencers

Want to be on the show?

The Lattice Inc show isn't limited to creators in the workforce or wellness space. This is an outlet for any influencer with a platform who wants to take their impact a step further — using their reach to give young people a real look at careers, hobbies, and resources that most people never encounter growing up.

If you have an audience and you care about what happens to the next generation — there's a role for you in this show. The only requirement is that you show up as yourself.
